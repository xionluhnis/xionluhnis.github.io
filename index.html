Homepage of Alexandre Kaspar

I am a PhD student in the Computational Fabrication Group of Professor Wojciech Matusik, working on crowdsourcing for graphics applications such as rotoscoping, as well as various other projects spanning fabrication, graphics, vision, machine learning and HCI.

Previously, I obtained a bachelor and master's degrees from EPFL where I worked in the Computer Graphics and Geometry laboratory of Professor Mark Pauly.
